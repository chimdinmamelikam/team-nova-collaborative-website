About Team Nova

Our Collaboration

Team Nova follows a real-world development workflow using Git and GitHub:

  • Each member works on their own branch
  • Pull requests are reviewed before merging
  • No direct pushes to the main branch
  • Team members contribute to commit history and documentation
  • Merge conflicts are resolved together as a learning experience

This project gave us a hands-on experience in teamwork, communication, and frontend development - skills we will apply in real client and professional environments.
